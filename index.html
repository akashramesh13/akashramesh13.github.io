About Me

I am a Platform Engineer at ADP India. But before I'm a talker, I'm a builder.

I don't care for unnecessary complexity. I build software that quietly does its job well—fast, offline-capable, and privacy-respecting. I prefer shipping finished products over accumulating half-built repositories.

My recent project, Aham, reflects this: no accounts, no cloud sync, no subscriptions. Just a pure journaling experience built on React Native and SQLite that runs anywhere.

I value the craftsmanship of taking an idea from a blank terminal to a polished product. When I'm not writing Java, React, or TypeScript, I'm probably optimizing my Arch setup.

Before software took over, I played chess competitively (South Zone Gold Medalist & National Runner-up). Secret? I still map out distributed systems like chess openings.
